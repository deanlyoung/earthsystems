  • 4th largest state
  • Mountains in the West and Plains in the East

  • 2nd largest veterans population
  • 48th most dense
  • Governor: Brian Schweitzer (D)
  • Lt. Gov.: John Bohlinger (R)
  • U.S. Senators: Max Baucus (D) and Jon Tester (D)

  • Demographics:
    • 92.7% White, 0.5% Black, 7.36% American Indian, 0.79% Asian, and 0.12% Pacific Islander
  • Gross Domestic Product (2009): $36 Billion (49th)
  • 36th highest per capita income (2009)
  • The economy is primarily based on agriculture, but lumber, mineral extraction, and tourism are important, too.
  • 2nd in most land in farms
