Carbon Limits and Energy for American Renewal Act - Cantwell-Collins
Cap-and-dividend, progressive carbon revenue return to the public
Senate Committee on Finance

  • The President sets the first cap on carbon
  • Producers and importers of coal, natural gas, and oil pay for permits
  • The cap becomes more stringent by .25% every year
  • Only 2,000 to 3,000 firms would be affected
  • Carbon permit prices are established by bidding every month
  • 75% of auction revenue goes back to consumers
  • 25% of auction revenue goes to the Clean Energy Reinvestment Trust Fund
