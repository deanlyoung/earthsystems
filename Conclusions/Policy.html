Cap-and-Dividend
Senator Tester would support it because it would not create a market for carbon.
Montanans would support it because it would rebate much of the costs.
Economists love it because it is a redistribution of capital as long as there are minimal transaction costs.
Energy suppliers prefer it because they can see the future price of carbon more easily, it is a cross between tax and quantity policies.
Environmentalists win because they like to see a cap on emissions.
