  • Issues: (from their website)
    • Support a federal program to reduce our dependence on petroleum and nuclear energy through the development of alternative sources such as ethanol, solar, and wind energy technologies among others to provide affordable energy.
    • Urge the use of all biomass, ethanol and biobased fuels as sources for strategic, transportation and heating fuels.
    • We encourage electric co-ops throughout the nation, to build and acquire the generating and transmission resources necessary to ensure the same balance of cost-based power.
    • Encourage continued monitoring and research of the Greenhouse Effect.
    • Any cap and trade system should address the following:
      1. Fair and equitable compensation for all agricultural producers to sequester carbon in an effort to reduce CO2 emissions.
      2. Exempt small U.S. refiners.
      3. Adequate alternative energy resources.
