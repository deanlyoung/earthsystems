  • Excerpt from The Dollars and Sense of Climate Change Legislation (by Webb Brown)
    • The results of the study were startling. The low-end estimates were that 4,000 to 6,000 Montanans could lose their jobs as a result of Lieberman-Warner by 2020. The benefits of any added jobs from new alternative energy production will be greatly overshadowed by jobs lost in other energy sectors. Additionally, the price of gasoline would increase between 74% and 140% by 2030, and the price of electricity would increase by 96% to 133% during that same time. Senator Joe Lieberman, a co-author of the bill, admitted the legislation would cost "hundreds of billions of dollars." These costs will be passed down the ladder to Montana families who are already concerned about high energy prices.
