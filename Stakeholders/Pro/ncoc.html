National Carbon Offset Coalition (carbon offset market)

  • NCOC Website
  • Making sustainable land use practices profitable: (from their website)
    • This site provides the information that you will need to earn income from the various conservation practices on your farm or ranch. Some of these practices you may already be doing, or you may be thinking of making changes to your cropping or land use practices. In either case, the annual sale of carbon credits may bring additional dollars to your operation, and also helps the environment.
    • Income Options:
      • No-Till
      • Seeded Grass Stands
      • Native Rangeland
      • Forestry
      • Methane Offset
    • NCOC's purpose is to work with the carbon credit program and act as a fiscal agent who is actually contracting and selling the carbon offsets on the Chicago Climate Exchange. Income earned from aggregating acres will result in revenue paid to individual landowners, and NCOC's to continue the good work of land stewardship.
